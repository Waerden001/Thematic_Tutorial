    "## Hirzebruch-Riemann-Roch theorem\n",
    "The Hirzebruch-Riemann-Roch formula states that the <a knowl=\"euler_characteristic\">Euler characteristic</a> $\\chi(X, E)$ of some <a knowl=\"coherent_sheaf\">coherent sheaf</a> $E$ on a smooth proper scheme $X$ over a field is given by the degree in the <a knowl=\"chow_group\">Chow group</a> of the dimension zero component of the product of the <a knowl=\"chern_character\">Chern character</a> $\\mathrm{ch}(E)$ of the sheaf and the <a knowl=\"todd_class\">Todd class</a> $\\mathrm{Td}_{X}$ of the tangent bundle of $X$.\n",
    "$$\\chi(X, E)=\\int_{X}\\mathrm{ch}(E)\\mathrm{Td}_{X}.$$"
